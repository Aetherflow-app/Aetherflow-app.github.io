Core Features in Action

📚 Prompt Library - Your AI Assistant Treasure

Feature Value: No more typing the same prompts repeatedly! Build your personal prompt library, categorize all quality prompts, and make every AI conversation more efficient.

💡 How to Use:

  • Click the extension icon to open the sidebar
  • Browse and manage in "My Prompts"
  • Use tags for categorization and quick search
  • Favorite frequently used prompts

📎 1-Click Save - Capture Great Prompts Instantly

Feature Value: Found a great prompt while browsing? Save it to your library with one click! Whether it's from social media, tech blogs, or AI tool websites, never miss good content again.

💡 How to Use:

  • Select the prompt text on any webpage
  • Right-click and choose "Save to Aetherflow"
  • Or use the shortcut Ctrl+Shift+S
  • Add tags and descriptions for easy management

⚡ Quick Input - Use Anywhere, Anytime

Feature Value: Whether on ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI platform, quickly insert your saved prompts. Say goodbye to copy-pasting and boost your productivity!

💡 How to Use:

  • Use shortcut Ctrl+Space in any input field
  • Select the prompt you need
  • Automatically inserts at current cursor position
  • Supports variable replacement and custom editing

🚀 Quick Optimize - AI-Powered Prompt Enhancement

Feature Value: Let AI help optimize your prompts! Through intelligent analysis, make your prompts clearer, more effective, and more likely to get satisfying responses.

💡 How to Use:

  • Select the prompt you want to optimize
  • Click the "Quick Optimize" button
  • AI automatically analyzes and provides optimization suggestions
  • Apply optimization results with one click

⚙️ Pro Optimize - Deep Customization for Your Prompts

Feature Value: More than simple optimization! Through the sidebar's professional tools, deeply analyze prompt structure and provide detailed improvement suggestions with multiple optimization options.

💡 How to Use:

  • Open the Aetherflow sidebar
  • Paste or input your prompt
  • Choose optimization goals (creativity, logic, efficiency, etc.)
  • Get detailed optimization analysis and suggestions
